/*Create a program that determines how many years you have left until retirement and the year you can retire.
It should prompt for your current age and the age you want to retire and display the output as shown in the
example below.
• What is your current age? 25
• At what age would you like to retire? 65
• You have 40 years left until you can retire.
• It’s 2024, so you can retire in 2064. */

let currentAge = parseInt(prompt("What is your current age?"));

let retireAge = parseInt(prompt("At what age would you like to retire?"));

let currentYear = 2024;

let yearsToRetirement = retireAge - currentAge;

let yearsToRetirementString = `You have ${yearsToRetirement} years left until you can retire.`;

let retireYear = yearsToRetirement + currentYear;

let retireYearString = `It’s ${currentYear}, so you can retire in ${retireYear}.`;

console.log(yearsToRetirementString);

console.log(retireYearString);
